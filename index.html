Introducing My Password Machine, a better way to manage your passwords.

My Password Machine creates a strong, unique password for every website.

You won't need to remember those passwords or write them down.

My Password Machine doesn't write down your passwords either! It just re-generates them on demand. That way there's no list of stored passwords for bad guys to steal.
