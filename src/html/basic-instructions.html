Instructions for My Password Machine


  1. Open My Password Machine.
  2. Enter the name of a website (for example, google.com or amazon.com).
  3. Enter your username for that website.
  4. Enter your master password.
  5. Click "Generate Password", and My Password Machine will give you a unique website password.
  6. Copy-and-paste that password into the website's login form.
  7. To get the same password again, just enter the same website name, username, and master password into My Password Machine.

    8. And you're done!
