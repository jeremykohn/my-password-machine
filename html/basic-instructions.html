My Password Machine - Instructions


  1. Open the login page of a website.
  2. Copy the web address of the login page.
  3. Paste that address into the "Website Name" field of My Password Machine.
  4. Enter your username for that website, along with your master password.
  5. Click "Generate Password", and My Password Machine will give you a unique website password.
  6. Copy-and-paste that password into the login page's "Password" field.
